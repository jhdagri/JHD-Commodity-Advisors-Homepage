About

James Day

Photo placeholder
James Day
Founder — JHD Commodity Advisors

James Day is an agricultural commodity professional with over 15 years of experience spanning physical bulk vessel grain trading, futures and options portfolio management, and commodity broking across global grain and oilseed markets.

A fourth-generation farmer, James brings ground-level production knowledge to his market analysis — an informational edge that institutional backgrounds rarely carry. His career has included managing grain procurement for one of the world's largest flour mills, running a discretionary commodity derivatives portfolio, and senior broking roles covering CBOT, MATIF, KCBT and Black Sea grain markets.

JHD Commodity Advisors was established to provide independent, commercially-driven advisory to producers, processors and trading firms who require specialist guidance without the conflicts inherent in bank or broker-led advice.
